## Overview

Topaz Photo AI Advanced is an all-in-one AI photo enhancement application that combines noise reduction, sharpening, and upscaling into a single intelligent workflow, automatically detecting which enhancements each image needs and applying them with a quality that no single-purpose tool can match.

Automatic subject detection identifies the main subject of each image and applies enhancement settings calibrated for that specific content type — faces, landscapes, architecture, or animals — rather than using generic settings that treat every image the same.

DEEP PRIME noise reduction uses deep learning trained on millions of images to separate genuine photographic detail from noise with an accuracy that traditional denoising algorithms cannot approach, producing clean images that retain sharpness and texture at high ISO settings.

AI upscaling generates realistic detail when enlarging images, making small originals usable at larger print sizes or display resolutions by synthesizing plausible high-frequency information rather than simply stretching existing pixels.

For photographers who need to maximize the quality of images captured in challenging conditions — high ISO, low light, compressed sources, or small originals — Topaz Photo AI Advanced is the enhancement tool that applies multiple quality improvements in the most intelligent and effective combination.

## Performance

Topaz Photo AI balances performance with system resource usage intelligently. Operations are handled in the background, keeping your Mac responsive for everything else. Efficient memory management and smart caching reduce overhead during extended work sessions.

Apple Silicon acceleration is fully utilized on M-series Macs, delivering noticeably faster performance. On Intel machines, hardware acceleration is used wherever available.
